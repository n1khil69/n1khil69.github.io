[ 01 / WHO I AM ]

Nothing breaks
a company faster.

I got into identity governance almost by accident — and stayed because nothing breaks a company faster than the wrong person with the wrong access.

I started at Wipro fresh from my CSE degree, where someone handed me a Saviynt instance and a migration nobody wanted. I shipped it. Two and a half years later I moved to Deloitte to do it for bigger clients, with messier estates and stricter auditors — EIC migrations, a dozen connector integrations, custom Java JARs when the platform said no.

Now at PwC AC, my bar hasn't changed: provisioning so automated nobody files a ticket, and audit trails so clean compliance reviews get boring. If out-of-the-box can't do it, I write the code that can.

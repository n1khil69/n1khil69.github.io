The Atelier

We began in a narrow room off the rue de Seine, with a drawing board and a single pot of ink.

L'Atelier de Fleurs is not a florist in the usual sense. We do not keep buckets of cut stems. We keep a library of drawings — every bloom rendered by the same hand, in the same line.

The table is yours. Place each flower where it wants to go, turn it, resize it, layer one behind another — and when the arrangement is just so, keep it as an image or send it on. The drawing is the gift.

— Camille Aubert, founder
